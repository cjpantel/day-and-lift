Our Story

Awesomedog started on the streets of Winnipeg over three decades ago. What began as one family's passion for great food and friendly service has grown into one of the city's most beloved institutions.

We've served customers through blizzards, heatwaves, Grey Cup parades, and everything in between. Rain or shine, you'll find us at the corner of Carlton and Portage, ready to serve.

Three decades. One family. One corner. That's Awesomedog.
